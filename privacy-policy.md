# Privacy Policy for Gladix

**Last updated: May 16, 2026**

Gladix ("the app") is a personal, open-source Android music player built on top of [Echo](https://github.com/brahmkshatriya/echo). This privacy policy explains what data is collected and how it is handled.

---

## The App Collects No User Data

Gladix itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app hosts zero content and does not operate any servers.

---

## Third-Party Services

Gladix integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

### Deezer
Gladix includes the Deezer extension, which connects to Deezer's servers to stream music. Your Deezer account credentials and listening activity are subject to [Deezer's Privacy Policy](https://www.deezer.com/legal/personal-datas).

### Firebase Crashlytics (Google)
Gladix uses Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports. This helps identify and fix stability issues. Crashlytics may collect:
- Device model and Android version
- App version
- Stack traces at the time of a crash
- Anonymous installation ID

No personally identifiable information is included in crash reports. You can review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more details.

### Additional Extensions
Users may optionally install additional extensions (such as Last.fm, EchoDown, etc.). Each extension connects to its own third-party service and is subject to that service's privacy policy.

---

## Data Storage

All app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to any Gladix-operated server.

Listening history is stored locally in a database on your device and is not shared with any third party.

---

## Children's Privacy

Gladix is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
