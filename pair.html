Link your TV

Enter the code shown on your TV and your Deezer ARL to link your account.

How do I find my ARL?
  1. Log into deezer.com in a desktop browser.
  2. Open DevTools with F12 (or right-click → Inspect).
  3. Go to Application → Cookies → https://www.deezer.com.
  4. Find the cookie named arl.
  5. Copy the value from the Value column — it's a long string of letters and numbers.
